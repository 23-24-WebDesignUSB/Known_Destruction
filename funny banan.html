BANANA INFORMATIVE

The banana is a fruit that is acshuly a berry in the subspeces of Musa sapientum and is native to Indomalaya and Australia. in some contrys bananas are called plantans and are mainly used for cooking. The world's largest producers of bananas in 2017 were India and China.





The leaves of banana plants are composed of a stalk (petiole) and a blade (lamina). The base of the petiole widens to form a sheath; the tightly packed sheaths make up the pseudostem, which is all that supports the plant.
